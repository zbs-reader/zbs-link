# A Library That Breathes

By dawn, the stair had led her to a wall of black stone shot through with ember veins. It rose from the clouds like a ship's hull, impossible and silent, until the silence broke.

The gate inhaled.

Dust peeled from the hinges. Bronze letters brightened one by one beneath Mira's fingertips, arranging themselves into a sentence she had not spoken aloud: **Archivists may enter. Liars must wait.**

She laughed despite herself, and the sound vanished into the seam as the door opened just wide enough to admit one frightened woman and the questions following at her heels.
