# Static Between Towers

Rain in Meridian did not fall so much as hover. It drifted between the towers in bright electric threads, carrying billboard light down into the alleys where couriers traded silence for speed.

Ren felt the memory chip twitch inside its insulated sleeve. The client had called it inactive. The client had also paid triple.

He ducked under a service awning and held the package to the glow of a cracked sign. For half a second, a face flickered in the mirrored foil.
